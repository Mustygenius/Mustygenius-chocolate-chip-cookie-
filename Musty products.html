INGREDIENTS

Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Unsalted butter (room temp) 1 cup
White granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Large eggs 2
Pure vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 2 cups

Optional Ingredients:

Ingredient Description
Nuts (walnuts, pecans, or hazelnuts) For added texture and flavor
Sea salt For sprinkling on top
Espresso powder For an intense flavor

Steps in Preparing Chocolate Chip Cookies

Step Instructions
1 Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
2 Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
3 Cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
4 Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
5 Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
6 Stir in chocolate chips.
7 Scoop tablespoon-sized balls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
8 Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
